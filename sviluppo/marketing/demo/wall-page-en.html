The most liveable cities of 2026: the ranking

by Editorial · 2 hours ago · 4 min read

The new quality-of-life survey rewards mid-sized cities, where services, environment and free time find the best balance.

According to the past year's data, the factors that weigh most are healthcare, transport and green spaces. Surprises abound, especially in the south.

Experts note how remote work has reshaped preferences: more and more people choose human-scale cities, away from big-city traffic.

In the coming days we will publish detailed profiles of each city, with scores per category and a comparison with last year.
