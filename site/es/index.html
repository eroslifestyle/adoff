Simulates standard ad variables. Site "sees" ads as loaded, even though they're not.

🎭
Fetch Interception

Intercepts fetch/XHR requests to detection servers. Responds with convincing fake data.

🪄
Bait Spoofing

Bait elements used to detect ad blockers are recognized and spoofed.

🌍
MAIN World Execution

Operates in the page's privileged context. Undetectable by standard extension isolation.

Replaces video ad SDK with a neutral stub. Player gets "no ads available".

🛡️
Anti-Fingerprinting

Masks extension traces in the DOM and browser APIs. Zero detectable signatures.
