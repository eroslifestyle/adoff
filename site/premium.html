FAQ

Questions about Premium

Does the 15-day free trial include the VPN?
The trial gives you the full AdBlock Pro experience: all ad blocking, video ad neutralization, and Stealth anti-detection. The VPN activates when you subscribe to Premium. This keeps the trial simple and the VPN cost-free until you decide.
Is the VPN inside the browser?
No. Browser-based VPN extensions have fundamental limitations: they can only route traffic from that specific browser tab, not from other apps or system services on your device. AdOff Premium uses dedicated mobile and desktop apps that tunnel all your device traffic — every app, every browser, every service. This is how real VPNs work.
What does the VPN log, exactly?
We log only: (1) whether your VPN account is active, and (2) the last timestamp you connected to the VPN (for the 7-day auto-disable feature). We do not log browsing history, visited sites, downloads, source/destination IPs, bandwidth, or any traffic data. Read the full VPN Policy for details.
What happens when my subscription ends?
When your Premium subscription ends or is cancelled, your VPN account is disabled immediately. The AdBlock Pro features you had active (trial or Pro) continue independently. Refunds are handled pro-rata for unused days — contact support.
Is P2P / torrenting allowed?
Yes. P2P and BitTorrent traffic are allowed on AdOff Premium VPN, including on dedicated P2P-optimized servers. All usage must be for lawful purposes. See the VPN Policy for the full terms.
Can I upgrade from Pro to Premium?
Yes. You can upgrade from Pro to Premium at any time. Your unused Pro days are credited pro-rata and applied to the Premium subscription cost. Contact support to upgrade, or use the upgrade option in your account dashboard.
What is the Founder Premium pool?
The first 100 Premium subscribers at the Founder price (€29.99 for the first year, then €49.99/year) get a dedicated Founder Premium badge and the locked-in first-year rate. This is a separate pool from the AdBlock Founder seats — you can be a Premium Founder even if you're not an AdBlock Founder.
How many devices can I use?
Up to 3 personal devices per Premium subscription. The VPN apps are available for Android (at launch) and desktop platforms (post-launch). The browser extension covers all your browsers regardless.
