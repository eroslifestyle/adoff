About AdOff

Standard Boilerplate

Use this short description when writing about AdOff.

AdOff is a privacy-first ad blocker for Chromium-based browsers, designed to block ads and trackers on all websites, with video ad neutralization on streaming platforms (Pro). Its exclusive Stealth Mode (Pro) makes it invisible to anti-adblock detection systems, making it one of the few browser extensions capable of bypassing modern anti-adblock walls without user intervention. Ultra-lightweight and built natively for Manifest V3, AdOff collects no user data. It's available free with a full 15 days of free trial trial, and offers paid Pro plans starting at EUR 2.69 per month or EUR 67.90 as a one-time Lifetime purchase. AdOff supports six languages and is available at adoff.app.
