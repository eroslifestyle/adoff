The Free tier is available to everyone without registration, payment, or account creation. It includes core ad blocking functionality as described above.

The Pro tier requires purchase of a license key. Pro features are activated by entering the license key in the extension. A Pro license allows simultaneous activation on up to 3 devices. Attempting to activate on more than 3 devices will require deactivating an existing device first.

3.3 Account system (optional)

Users may optionally create an AdOff account to manage their devices and licenses from a single dashboard at adoff.app/account. Account creation is not required for any tier, including Pro.

Available sign-in methods:

  • Google Sign-In , sign in with your Google account
  • Email + password , register with an email address and password
  • License key + email , sign in using your Pro license key and purchase email (no password needed)

Account data is governed by our Privacy Policy. The Free version requires no account and involves no server communication.

3.4 Device management

Each browser installation counts as one device. Pro users can view and manage their active devices from adoff.app/account. To stay within the 3-device limit, users may remove devices from their account dashboard. A maximum of 3 device removals per day applies to prevent abuse.
